STEPS TO PREPARE HYDERABADI CHICKEN BIRYANI

  1. Ingredients Preparation
    1. 2 cups Basmati Rice (soaked for 30 minutes)
    2. 500 grams Chicken (washed and cut into pieces)
    3. 1 cup Yogurt (curd)
    4. 2 large Onions (sliced thin)
    5. 2 Tomatoes (chopped)
    6. 2 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
    7. Whole spices (bay leaf, cardamom, cinnamon, cloves)
    8. 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder
    9. 1 tsp Red Chili Powder
    10. 1 tsp Biryani Masala or Garam Masala
    11. Fresh coriander and mint leaves (chopped)
    12. Saffron soaked in warm milk (optional)
    13. Salt to taste
    14. Oil & Ghee for cooking
  2. COOKING HYDERABADI CHICKEN BIRYANI
    1. Marinate the Chicken:
      • In a bowl, mix chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric, red chili powder, salt, and biryani masala.
      • Add some chopped mint and coriander leaves.
      • Let it marinate for at least 1 hour.
    2. Fry the Onions:
      • Heat oil/ghee in a pan and fry the sliced onions till golden brown (crispy).
      • Set some fried onions aside for garnishing.
    3. Prepare the Rice:
      • Boil water with whole spices and salt.
      • Add soaked rice and cook until 70% done (not fully cooked).
      • Drain and keep aside.
    4. Layer and Cook Biryani:
      • In a heavy-bottomed pot, add marinated chicken as the first layer.
      • Add a layer of half-cooked rice on top of the chicken.
      • Sprinkle fried onions, chopped herbs, saffron milk on top.
      • Repeat layers if needed and cover with a tight lid.
      • Cook on low heat (dum) for 20â€“25 minutes.
    5. Serve:
      • Let it rest for 10 minutes before opening the lid.
      • Serve hot with raita, salan, or boiled eggs.
